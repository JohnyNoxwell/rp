Web
Task
To create a portal for video uploading of empty megacities streets during pandemic.
Solution
A solution was proposed and implemented with video encoding on-the-fly, support with vtt-subtitles, which made it possible to dynamically adapt the video to the channel width and merge various parts of the video into one continuous clip. CMS strapi is used to load content; the client part is implemented in nuxt. Mp4 repackager selected as video source.

Non-commercial project
Fancy Shot | Empty Cities
In 2020, the world unexpectedly faced a global pandemic. Residents of almost all countries were forced to maintain self-isolation and cities were empty. Fancy Shot decided to take this unique opportunity to collect videos of empty megacities.
Quick start
Using a CMS allowed us quickly create a content publishing solution to focus on optimizing video output. Streaming video made it possible to use the site even on mobile devices with an unstable Internet connection.
